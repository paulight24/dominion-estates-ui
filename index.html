Secure & Flexible Payment Methods

At Dominion Estates, we prioritize guest safety and peace of mind. To secure your reservation, guests pay a refundable $500 reservation deposit.

Payments are processed securely via Square or KeyCheck. We do not store or process card numbers on our site, ensuring your data is completely secure.

Payment Schedule & Terms:

  • Reservation is officially secured upon receipt of the $500 refundable deposit.
  • The deposit acts as a security hold and is fully refundable post check-out, pending unit inspection.
  • The remaining rental balance is due in full exactly one week prior to arrival, unless otherwise agreed in writing.
