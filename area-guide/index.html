Good to Know

  • Uber & Lyft are readily available across the area.
  • John Wayne Airport (SNA) is about 10–15 minutes away.
  • Bring sunscreen year-round — the coastal sun is strong.
  • Parking fills quickly at popular beaches; arrive early on weekends.
  • Sunset is one of the best times to explore the coast.
  • Weekdays are generally far less crowded than weekends.
